### Question 1: Multiple Choice
What is the key distinction between Physical AI and traditional AI?

A) Physical AI uses more sensors than traditional AI
B) Physical AI operates in a closed loop with the physical environment, while traditional AI operates in virtual spaces
C) Physical AI is faster than traditional AI
D) Physical AI uses different programming languages

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>
**Correct Answer: B** - Physical AI operates in a closed loop with the physical environment, while traditional AI operates in virtual spaces. This is the fundamental distinction between the two approaches.
</details>

---
