# Quizzes: Introduction to Physical AI

## Quiz 1.1: Physical AI Fundamentals

### Question 1
What is the key difference between traditional AI and Physical AI?

A) Physical AI uses more data than traditional AI
B) Physical AI operates in physical environments and must interact with real-world physics
C) Physical AI is faster than traditional AI
D) Physical AI uses different programming languages

**Correct Answer: B**

### Question 2
Which of the following is NOT a key characteristic of Physical AI?

A) Embodied Cognition
B) Real-time Processing
C) Abstract Symbol Manipulation
D) Multi-sensory Integration

**Correct Answer: C**

### Question 3
What does "embodied cognition" mean in the context of Physical AI?

A) The AI system has a physical body
B) Intelligence emerges from the interaction between the system and its physical environment
C) The AI system can recognize human bodies
D) The AI system is embodied with multiple sensors

**Correct Answer: B**

### Question 4
Which middleware is commonly used as the foundation for Physical AI systems?

A) MQTT
B) ROS 2 (Robot Operating System 2)
C) Apache Kafka
D) ZeroMQ

**Correct Answer: B**

### Question 5
What is the "reality gap" in Physical AI?

A) The gap between different AI models
B) The difference between simulated and real-world performance
C) The gap between training and testing data
D) The difference between AI and human intelligence

**Correct Answer: B**

### Question 6
Which of the following is a critical safety consideration for Physical AI systems?

A) Data privacy
B) Cybersecurity
C) Fail-safe mechanisms
D) All of the above

**Correct Answer: D**

### Question 7
What is the main purpose of simulation in Physical AI development?

A) To replace real-world testing completely
B) To provide a safe and cost-effective environment for testing and training
C) To make development more complex
D) To reduce the need for sensors

**Correct Answer: B**

### Question 8
Which of the following is NOT typically part of an action-perception loop?

A) Sensing the environment
B) Processing sensor data
C) Taking actions in the environment
D) Storing data in the cloud

**Correct Answer: D**
